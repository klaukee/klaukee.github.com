LUNAR GALA

INTERACTION DESIGN


A digital program designed and built to accompany Carnegie Mellon University's student run fashion show, Lunar Gala.


The Lunar Gala fashion show is an incredible display of art, design and tech. Students from every discipline of the university come together to design and model fashion lines created entirely by students. We created a web app that showcased the impressive work put into this annual fashion event.

Designed in collaboration with Max Hawkins


See it live

During the show, the audience was able to access the web app on their phones. As the audience was getting seated and waiting for the opening of the show, they were able to read info about the designers and models and view the event sponsors.

When the show began, we pushed updates to the app to display a gif and information of each of the looks as they came down the runway.


Capturing the looks

Tech played an important role in that year's event. We collaborated with the Carnegie Mellon Robotic's Institute to use their newly developed 360 camera to capture images of each look.


Each recording contained hundreds of 2D images that could then be constructed into a single 3D image, as seen in the point cloud example. The Robotics Institute was able to use this data to continue to develop the technology.
