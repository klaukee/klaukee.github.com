Prana is a beautiful solution for active people who are on-the-go, but need to take daily medications. The system consists of a pill box with embedded smart technology that communicates with the mobile app. The app allows you to manage perscriptions and set alarms so that you medications aren't another chore.

Designed in collaboration with Chris Ioffreda

A digital experience

Add your medications and set daily reminders using the Prana app. Prana learns more and more about your medication habits and will alert you to take your pills at the optimal time on both your smartphone and the Prana pillbox.
