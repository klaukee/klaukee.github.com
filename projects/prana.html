PRANA

INDUSTRIAL & INTERACTION DESIGN


For active people who are on-the-go, taking medications shouldn't be an added chore. Prana's smart pill box works alongside the mobile app to reinforce good pill taking habits.


For young adults, coping with an illness while trying to live an active, healthy lifestyle can be very challenging. In many cases, taking the medication on time and as prescribed is crucial for its effectiveness. Prana supports healthy medication routines with physical and digital reminder system.

Designed in collaboration with Chris Ioffreda.

Make it a habit

Add medications through the mobile app to set weekly, daily and hourly reminders. Assign medications to the pill pods to tell the system how frequently you need to take them. The pills are color coded in correlation to the light that will illuminate on the physical pill pods when it's time to take the meds.


Many products on the market that address medication adherence are digital solutions, but we believe that these digital habits are inconsistent. Prana's pill box reminds you to take your medication with sound and color coded lights. This is more effective than a phone alarm that users can disable and forget about.



Take it to-go

All light and sound reminders work when the pods are out of the charging station. Take them to work or on a weekend trip by separating them from the base and snapping them together with the magnetic sides.



Customize your Prana

Make a pill box that fits your habits, but purchasing pods with several divider options. It comes in the standard single pod, double and triple to better organize your medication.
