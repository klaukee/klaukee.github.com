PAYTANGO

STARTUP FOR BIOMETRIC PAYMENTS


In the Fall of 2012, I founded PayTango with Brian Groudan , Christian Reyes and Umang Patel. We developed our biometric payment startup in YCombinator's 2013 winter batch.


PayTango makes payments easy by allowing you to associate the cards in your wallet with your fingerprint. The PayTango terminal transmits fingerprints as card data that existing systems already understand. Registration takes mere seconds - place two fingers on the fingerprint reader, swipe your card, and enter a phone number. To pull up your account and complete a transaction, just place two fingers on the reader.


Building the product from the ground up

As the Chief Product Officer (although we all had a hand in everything) I lead the development of our physical product. I designed, built and oversaw the production of our payment terminals. In addition to my industrial design contributions, I also was heavily involved in the company branding and UX design.
