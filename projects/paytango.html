PAYTANGO

STARTUP FOR BIOMETRIC PAYMENTS


A biometric payments startup founded alongside Brian Groudan , Christian Reyes and Umang Patel. Developed in YCombinator's winter 2013 batch.


To make everyday transactions, we carry around over half a dozen cards and are reliant on an inefficient payments system. We created PayTango, a terminal that associates your fingerprints with the cards in your wallet, to create a system that is convenient for both customers and vendors.


We were incredibly fortunate to participate in YCombinator in Mountainview, CA. As the Chief Product Officer (although we all had a hand in everything) I lead the development of our physical product. I designed, built and oversaw the production of our payment terminals. In addition to my industrial design contributions, I also was heavily involved in the company branding and UX design.
