As a user experience designer with YouTube, I've had the opportunity to work on projects that range from the watch page, to music, to video ads.

Playlists that delight

With the release of YouTube Music, playlists have gained elevated importance on the platform. As the UX owner, I focused on ensuring that playlists would support a seamless music experience across our platforms. This included unifying and refreshing the playlist experience across our deivces and product features.
