SAAB EMERGENCY RADIO

Industrial Design (Fall 2011)

This project was an excersize in brand consistency as well as designing for a existing company's target audience. I designed an emergency radio, which was visually complimentary to the Saab 9-5. Before the design process I conducted research alongside Mike Bolster and Conrad Ennis. We studied and explored Saab's visual history, the company's target demographic and the styling of the 9-5. We compiled and synthesized this research in a style guide, which would then inform the companion products we designed independently.
